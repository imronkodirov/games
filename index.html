🕹️ Game Instructions

Game 1. The Century Flip (Grand Finale)

Set-up: One thick hardcover book (minimum 400 pages).

Action: Student gets one flip to land on Page 100.

Win:

  • Exact Page 100 = T-Shirt
  • Pages 95–105 = Tote Bag

Strategy: This is harder than it looks. Hype every attempt.

Game 2. The 7-Second Word Hunt (Fast Control Window)

Set-up: One textbook or dense novel + a stopwatch.

Action: Give the player one target word. They have 7 seconds to point to it on a random page.

Win: They find the word before the timer hits zero.

Prize: Pen

Strategy: Keep the timer strict to protect the pen supply.

Game 3. Blind Book Sketch (Crowd Builder)

Set-up: Handheld whiteboard + marker.

Action: Student closes their eyes and has 15 seconds to draw a prompt.

Win: If the next person in line guesses correctly, the artist wins.

Prize: Notebook

Strategy: Funny drawings attract a crowd quickly.

Game 4. Triple Shelf Challenge (Fast Category Quiz)

Set-up: Prepare 4–5 category cards with 5–6 short questions in each category.

Action: Student chooses and answers three questions from three different categories.

Rule: The same category cannot be used twice in one round.

Win: Student answers all three correctly.

Prize: Pen

Strategy: Keep questions short, rotate categories, and move quickly. This works best as a fast verbal quiz with visible category options.

🗄️ The Content Vault

Game 4 — Triple Shelf Challenge Categories

Suggested Categories:
  • Fantasy
  • Uzbek Literature
  • World Classics
  • Library Facts
  • Famous Characters
Use 4–5 categories total. Prepare 5–6 short questions under each.
Suggested Rule Script:

“Choose three different categories. You’ll get one question from each. Answer all three correctly and you win.”

Question Writing Notes:
  • Keep questions short and answerable in 5–10 seconds.
  • Mix easy and medium questions.
  • Avoid categories that need too much cultural background.
  • Do not repeat the same category in one round.

Game 3 — Blind Sketch Prompts

  • A giant pencil
  • A graduation cap

🚩 Peer Instructions (10:20 – 10:55)

Script:

“Welcome to the Word Hunt! You have 7 seconds to find one word in this book. If you find it, you win a pen. One try per person. Go!”

  • Keep the timer strict: exactly 7 seconds.
  • If they lose, give a bookmark/sticker if available and say: “Almost! Check the library for more secrets later!”
  • Do not give out more than 4 pens during this window.
