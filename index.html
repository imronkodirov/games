🕹️ Quick Game Guide

#1 The Century Flip

Action: One flip to land on Page 100. Hardcover book only.

Win: Page 100 (T-Shirt) | Pages 95–105 (Tote).

#2 The 7-Second Word Hunt (Peer Window)

Action: Student has 7 seconds to find a specific word on a dense page.

Win: Success = Pen. (Limit to 4 pens max during this window!)

#7 Blind Book Sketch

Action: Close eyes. 15 seconds to draw a prompt on the whiteboard.

Win: If the next person in line guesses it correctly = Notebook.

#8 Mystery Genre Guess

Action: Read the first line. 5 seconds to name the genre.

Win: Correct Genre = Pen.

🗄️ The Content Vault

Genre Lines:
  • "The ship broke the light-speed barrier." (Sci-Fi)
  • "Blood on the snow was the first thing the detective noticed." (Mystery)
  • "Dragon-fire is not easily extinguished." (Fantasy)
  • "The ghost of my father sat at the table." (Horror/Magical Realism)
Word Hunt Targets: Hypothesis, Nevertheless, Equilibrium, Quantitative, Specifically.
